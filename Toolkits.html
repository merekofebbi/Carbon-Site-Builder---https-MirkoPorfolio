Our Toolkits

Climate acuity is rooted in impact-focused academic research. We want to be as transparent as we can about our evidence, tools, and methods. We open-source wherever possible.

Learn more ▼

Our Digital Sustainability Game is the core of our digital sustainability workshops. Get in touch if you'd like us to run a session. Or with a little preparation, you can can run the game within your organization, or deliver to external audiences. The game is open source under a permissive CC license, so you can even adapt it to create your own versions.

We have also contributed to the following:

Communicating Climate Risk: A Toolkit is a comprehensive interdisciplinary guide to communicating climate risk across science, business, finance, policy, education, and community contexts.

The Climate Futures Storytelling Collection. This includes:

  • Kampala Yénkya
  • Kampala Yénkya Multiverse
  • Creating With Uncertainty
  • The Serious Games Cookbook
  • Climate Uncertainty and the Arts

The Digital Humanities Climate Coalition Toolkit is an open source, community-led project with a focus on improving the environmental impacts of digital technologies within the digital humanities, the arts and humanities, and academia and cultural heritage organizations generally.
